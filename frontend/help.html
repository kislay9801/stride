Help & Guide

How each part of Stride works.

dashboard

Dashboard

Your day at a glance: a timeline of tasks, your weekly focus chart, an AI insight, and your cognitive load. Use the + button to add a task; hover a task to mark it done or delete it.

assignment_turned_in

Assignment Breakdown

Paste a brief or upload a PDF/text file, then Generate Roadmap — AI turns it into ordered steps with estimates and due dates. Add, edit, complete, or delete steps anytime.

auto_stories

Revision Hub

Create a notebook and paste your notes. Click Generate Study Materials and AI builds flashcards, a quiz, and summaries from those notes — all at once. Flip cards, take the quiz, or add cards by hand.

psychology

AI Tutor

Chat with an AI study companion — ask for explanations, get quizzed, or plan a study session. It knows what subjects you're working on.

Good to know

  • monitoringCognitive Load reflects your current workload — it rises with pending tasks and falls as you complete them. Lower means a lighter day.
  • notificationsReminders live behind the bell in the top bar — today's tasks and upcoming assignment steps.
  • searchSearch (top bar) finds your tasks, notebooks, and assignments.
  • dark_modeDark mode and your profile live in Settings.
